The Clymb

Mobile Study / 5 weeks

HOW IT ALL BEGAN

The Clymb is a very diverse website, with rich content and an online store. I am on this website constantly looking for something new. However, they have no mobile presence, and since I’m moving all the time I can’t browse their website as much as I’d like. That’s where this concept comes in. I wanted to create a mobile application and focus on just my favorite feature: the Stories.

What to bring over

GEAR LOVE

Professionals and experts discuss products listed on the website. They go into detail with where they used it and how they used it, so you know what you’re getting.

OUT THERE

The real meaty part of the Stories feature is where all of the good reading is. Out There showcases the outdoor culture through its users, along with professionals and experts.

BUYING GUIDES

Nothing’s worse than not being prepared for a big trip. These guides will help make the best decisions.
