City Root

Data visualization / 6 weeks

HOW FAR WILL YOUR SALARY TAKE YOU?

We move to somewhere new for different reasons: career, promotions, family. Making this move to a new city can be a scary feeling. What’s even scarier is not being fully prepared for what’s coming. Cost of living is something that everyone should consider when moving to another city.

WHAT THE USER WANTS

What I really wanted to know is what the user actually needed to make the best decision. Using sources such as CNN Money, Zumper, and surveying many people, I’ve gathered the most useful categories for any user that is looking at cost of living.

    Key Features
  • Rent
  • Groceries
  • Insurance
  • Transportation
  • Gas

Approach

VISUAL DISPLAY

Working with this kind of information, the visual data needed to be clear and easy to understand. There were many iterations, but it all came down to a few.

MAKING AN EASY MOVE

Moving somewhere new is never easy. There are many things to consider and a lot of costs to cover. This application shows the data that’s necessary and compares everything to your current city, just so you know what to expect. It’s a tool that’s used for help make your next big chapter in life as easy and frictionless as possible.
