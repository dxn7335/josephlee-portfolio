Product Design / 20 weeks

HOW CAN WE BRING PEOPLE MORE MONEY?

Twice a year, Intuit holds a hackathon called Codechella in their San Diego office. Teams consisted of engineers, designers, and program managers. We were all given one problem: “How can we bring people more money?”.

IDEATION AND RESEARCH

After the hackathon event, senior leadership wanted us to pursue our idea and have our team continue working on this project. We gathered and regrouped to have a better approach on how to fully build this application. This is when focused on what features were necessary and how we would implement them for our users.

KEY TAKEAWAYS

CURATED CONTENT

We didn’t want our users to feel overwhelmed with items they didn’t want. Our team decided that we would have them choose what they can see so that they never feel like they’re advertisements.

SMALL BUSINESSES

One great reason working with Intuit is the ecosystem that they have. We wanted to utilize the customers on QuickBooks Online and help promote their small businesses through our application.

SAVE MORE MONEY

In the end, our main goal was to help people put more money back into their pockets. We wanted to help inform our users of every dollar they could save and be able to manage it from there.
